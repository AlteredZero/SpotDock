# SpotDock

SpotDock is a lightweight floating Spotify companion designed for fast and seamless music control without opening the full Spotify app. It features a draggable always-on-top mini dock that displays the currently playing song, album art, artist name, and playback controls, while expanding into a compact music panel with queue, library access, and quick controls. Built for productivity and multi-monitor setups, SpotDock keeps your music accessible anywhere on screen with a clean, modern, and distraction-free interface.
